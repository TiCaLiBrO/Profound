
# Concepts

Some of the cool ideas in the game:

It can be a bit like Dark Souls, except there's only one, singular bonfire. In that, when you die, you always wake up in the exact same place in the exact same way.
The difference is that you have new knowledge that can take you further.
You have profound knowledge that can be used as a shortcut. As well as making physical shortcuts by opening doors from the other side, kicking down ladders, tying ropes, or killing other people.
Some of the world's changes may be permanent - though I don't yet have the rules for that figured out. Perhaps you have the ability later on to make persistent changes.

Actually, perhaps there is no way to save the game, and everything relies on profound knowledge.
